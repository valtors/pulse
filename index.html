what it is

a personal ai agent that lives on your machine. not in the cloud. not on someone's server. yours.

it remembers you

sqlite on disk. survives restarts. it knows your focus, your projects, what you care about and what you ignore. tell it once. it knows forever. or until you tell it to forget.

it connects accounts

github is wired. gmail and calendar are next. each connector feeds the same pipeline. add a service, pulse learns from it.

it thinks

bring your own key. any openai-compatible provider. without an llm, pattern matching. with one, it reasons about your data, answers questions, writes summaries.

it stays yours

your data never leaves your machine. no telemetry. no phone home. no account. sqlite on disk, config in json, one binary. that's the whole thing.
