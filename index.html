pulse
connect everything. your ai does the rest.

a personal ai agent that lives on your machine. it connects to your accounts, remembers what matters to you, and cuts through the noise. not a cloud service. not a dashboard. a binary you run.

what it does

remembers you

sqlite memory persists across restarts. it knows your focus, your projects, what you care about and what you ignore. every interaction makes it smarter about you.

connects accounts

github is wired. gmail and calendar are stubbed. each connector plugs into the same pipeline. add a service, pulse learns from it.

thinks with llm

openai-compatible. bring your own key, any provider. without an llm, pulse does pattern matching. with one, it reasons about your data, answers questions, and writes summaries.

runs locally

your data never leaves your machine. no telemetry, no phone home, no account. sqlite on disk, config in json, one binary.

architecture

rust core

rust

filtering engine, sqlite memory, llm orchestration, digest builder. serde, rusqlite, reqwest. the brain.

go shell

go

http server, web ui, cli, connector management. stdlib only. the hands.

go calls rust. they share sqlite and json. no grpc, no protobuf, no microservices. two languages because each does what it's best at.
